Sonic Sonic is a fast, blue, heroic, hedgehog who rolls at the speed of light. He saves his world from threats like Dr Eggman, The Deadly Six, Chaos, Dark Gaia, and a lot more. With Dr Eggman being his most common threat. He is able to defeat these kinds of creatures with the power of the Chaos Emeralds, which transforms him into Super Sonic
